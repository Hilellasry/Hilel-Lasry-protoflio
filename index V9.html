About

Hillel Lasry is a visual storyteller specializing in cinematic film production and photography. With over a decade of experience crafting compelling visual narratives, Hillel's work spans commercial projects, documentaries, music videos, and fine art photography.

His distinctive approach combines technical precision with an intuitive understanding of emotional resonance, creating visuals that not only capture attention but also forge lasting connections with audiences.

Based in Los Angeles but working globally, Hillel collaborates with brands, agencies, musicians, and fellow artists who value authentic storytelling and visual excellence.
